Introduction

Star Wars is the science fiction odyssey that has taken the mainstream audiences by storm

Unfortunately, many older fans fervently claim that only the original trilogy is worth watching and they actively campaign against the newer iterations. Conversely, more reasonable fans and new comers a like are just happy to see the Star Wars universe continue to expand with the addition of new content

Since the order of the films can be confusing to new comers this page can be used as a reference to help you understand the sequence of events within the canonical story line.

Please review the summaries below. All items in the section below are listed in order of release date.

release date: May 25, 1977

Film Overview

This episode introduces the audience to the main cast Luke Skywalker, Han solo and princess Leia. The film begins with princess Leia under attack by the evil Darth Vader. We quickly learn that she is part of the rebellion against the oppressive empire and has received secret plans that will falcate the destruction of the Death star. The plans land on the planet Tatooine via the droid (R2-D2). Eventually Luke Skywalker acquires these droids and his life begins to change rapidly. He soon discovers that he is the son of a Jedi warrior and begins his quest to learn the ways of the force and understand his place in the universe.

Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back

release date: May 21, 1980

Original Trailer

Film Overview

Episode five returns fan to the amazing Star Wars universe. This episode picks up after the defeat of the empire at the battle of Yavin IV that resulted in the destruction of the Death Star. The film begins with Darth Vader searching for Luke Skywalker and his friends. Vader seeks to destroy the rebel base and force Luke to join him as an evil sith lord to rule the galaxy. Vader eventual finds Luke on the planet Hoth but he is able to escape after the battle along with the rest of the rebellion. Luke venture to the Dagoba system where he meets a mysterious former Jedi who will train him in the ways of the force. Meanwhile Han Solo and Princess Leia attempt to evade capture form the ruthlessly persistent pursuit of Darth Vader.
