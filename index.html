Introduction

Star Wars is the science fiction odyssey that has taken the mainstream audiences by storm

Unfortunately, many older fans fervently claim that only the original trilogy is worth watching and they actively campaign against the newer iterations. Conversely, more reasonable fans and new comers a like are just happy to see the Star Wars universe continue to expand with the addition of new content

Since the order of the films can be confusing to new comers this page can be used as a reference to help you understand the sequence of events within the canonical story line.
